Chasing the 10 Pound Monster is a weight loss challenge web app. The objective is to be the first player to lose 10 pounds. Players will enter their weight weekly into the web app. The web app will show only the gain or loss , not the actual weight, for players.

10 pounds is an attainable, measureable, and visible fitness goal. The best part is it can be done in a short time period . So choose, do you want or need to chase the 10 Pound Monster?
